Ball Theory is a basketball research community that focuses on the social and mental aspects and explores ways of engaging with new perspectives of the game.

About

Each basketball game is a process of collective decision making. Every action is interpreted by the players to inform the next step of a play and so to reduce the uncertainty about the steps that follow in the sequence of coordinated decisions, to resolve game situations through the management of space in the court.

Basketball is a form of expression, its interaction conforms a great context for social and emotional development. And why not, thanks to Ball Theory, for philosophical, socio-cultural and scientific understanding as well.
